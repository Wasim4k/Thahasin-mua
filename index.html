About Thahasin

Where tradition meets artistry

With over 5 years of professional experience, Thahasin is Trichy's trusted name for bridal and traditional makeup. Trained in classical Indian bridal aesthetics, she brings out the natural beauty of every bride while honouring the rich traditions of Tamil Nadu.

From intimate temple weddings to grand receptions, Thahasin's steady hand and artistic eye ensure you feel confident, radiant, and completely yourself on your most cherished day.

Serving brides across Trichy, Chennai, and beyond — bringing the studio to you.
